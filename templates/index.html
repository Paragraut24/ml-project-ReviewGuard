How it Works
Two powerful models working in tandem to analyze both the language and behavioral patterns of any review.
Step 01

Text Input

Paste any product review. The system preprocesses and tokenizes the text for downstream analysis.

Step 02

BERT Analysis

Features
Why ReviewGuard?
Built with state-of-the-art NLP and graph learning to handle even the most sophisticated fake reviews.

Linguistic Pattern Detection

BERT understands context, not just keywords. It catches subtle signals like vague superlatives, over-positive sentiment, and unnatural sentence structures.

Behavioral Graph Analysis

The GNN layer models reviewer networks to spot bots, coordinated fake review campaigns, and suspicious posting patterns.

Real-Time Inference

Results are returned in under a second, making it suitable for live e-commerce platforms and browser extension integration.

Heuristic Safety Net

Rule-based boosters catch obvious spam patterns like exclamation mark overload and ALL CAPS abuse that pure ML might under-score.

Transparent Scoring

Platform Agnostic
